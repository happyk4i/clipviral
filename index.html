Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data Collection

ClipViral collects only the minimum data necessary to operate: your TikTok OAuth tokens (stored locally on your machine), and the video files you explicitly select for processing.

2. TikTok Data

We access your TikTok account only through the OAuth scopes you authorize (user.info.basic and video.upload). No TikTok data is stored on our servers, sold, or shared with third parties.

3. Local Processing

Video processing (audio analysis, transcription, clipping) happens entirely on your local machine. No video content is sent to external servers except the final clip you choose to upload to TikTok via the Content Posting API.

4. Third Parties

We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The only external service is the TikTok API, which you control through OAuth.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored in a local .env file on your machine. You can revoke them at any time via your TikTok Developer settings.

Privacy concerns should be directed to the developer.
